NYU⋅ME | UX/UI _ App

Logo concept: Nyu⋅Me (british pronunciation of New Me)

NyuMe is an App which offers a collection of simple remedies and recipes using common natural ingredients, designed for people who like to rely on natural products for their beauty routine.

Problem Statement

People who prefer to use natural ingredients for their beauty care consider organic products well working but expensive. People is busy so they would prefer to do not waste time and get information about natural skin/hair care hacks and tips possibly from one source to find not complicated remedies.

Competitor Analysis
Direct and indirect competitors to find out the already existing alternatives in the market segment. The major are represented by three apps.
I profiled each one analising:

  • Key objectives
  • Overall strategy
  • Market advantage
  • Market target
  • Marketing strategies
  • Products/Services offered
  • Pricing & cost

User research & Data analysis

I surveyed 94 people in target (quantitative research) asking questions about their beauty routine, how much time they spend on it, which skin problem they care most. After analysing the collected data I created 3 Personas and the corresponding user journeys.

Information architecture

(Sitemap)

To define how content will be structured and presented to users when they are interacting with the design.

Wireframing

Through the Sketching technique I established the basic structure of the pages and the hierarchy of the content.

User flow

The users interaction through the two available paths, choosing between:

  • Element (ingredient)
  • Treatment

Usability test

I conducted the usability test on 5 users in target, asking them to complete 3 tasks.
Then I analysed:

  • Average time on every screen/action
  • Heatmap
  • Path chosen

I then made the necessary changes.

Soft UI | Neumorphism

I chose to use Neumorphism because I wanted to make the colorful products (ingredients) stand out in a monochromatic environment (interface) and let the contrast and shadow levels typical of Soft Ui show CTAs or interactive components.
